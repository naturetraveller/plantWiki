Flora Europaea

Edited by James Cullen, Sabina G. Knees, H. Suzanne Cubey. A comprehensive and detailed compilation of the flora of Europe, providing information on thousands of plant species.

Flora of North America

Edited by Flora of North America Editorial Committee. An extensive series of volumes documenting and describing the plant species of North America.

The Plant Book

Edited by D. J. Mabberley. A comprehensive reference containing information on over 10,000 plant species and considered a standard work in botanical literature.

Flora of China

Edited by Wu Zheng-yi, Peter H. Raven, Deyuan Hong. A multi-volume work extensively documenting the flora of China and serving as one of the most important botanical references for China.

The Jepson Manual: Vascular Plants of California

Edited by Bruce G. Baldwin, Douglas Goldman, David J. Keil. A comprehensive manual describing the vascular plants of California and considered the authoritative reference for the plant life of this region.

The Sibley Guide to Trees

By David Allen Sibley. An illustrated field guide providing detailed information on North American tree species and known for its precise illustrations.

The New Royal Horticultural Society Dictionary of Gardening

Edited by Anthony Huxley, Mark Griffiths, Margot Levy. A comprehensive reference containing information on garden plants from around the world, published by the Royal Horticultural Society.

Flora: An Illustrated History of the Garden Flower

By Brent Elliott. A lavishly illustrated book exploring the history and significance of garden flowers in various eras and cultures.
