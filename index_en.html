About this Page

Welcome to my index page on the systematics of plants in Central Switzerland. On this page, I will gradually present the systematics of plants in this region. My focus will initially be on the plants native to Central Switzerland.

Systematics of Plants

Here, I will explain the fundamental levels of plant systematics, starting from the Kingdom and going through various taxonomic ranks such as Classes, Orders, Families, Genera, and Species.

Plants in Central Switzerland

In this section, I will present a selection of plant species that are native to Central Switzerland. I will provide information about their characteristics, distribution areas, and, if applicable, their ecological significance.

Resources

In this section, I will list useful resources such as books, websites, and databases that offer additional information on the systematics of plants and plant species in Central Switzerland.
